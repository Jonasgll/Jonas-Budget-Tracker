## Etsy Listing

### Title

Budget Tracker App | Personal Finance Planner, Expense & Savings Goal Tracker, Debt Manager, PWA

### Description

Take complete control of your money with this beautifully designed Budget Tracker web app — your all-in-one personal finance tool that works on any device, installs like a native app, and keeps your financial life organized in minutes.

**What you get:**
- **Dashboard** with real-time KPIs: income, expenses, savings, and balance at a glance
- **Income & Expense Tracking** with categorized entries (Rent, Groceries, Transport, Health, and more)
- **Savings Goals** with visual progress bars — track every goal from vacation funds to emergency savings
- **Debt Tracker** to monitor what you owe and celebrate every payment milestone
- **Annual Overview** with month-by-month comparison charts so you always see the big picture
- **Chart Visualizations** — donut chart by category + 6-month bar chart trend
- **Multi-currency support**: €, $, £, CHF
- **Dark & Light mode** for comfortable use day or night
- **CSV Export** to back up or analyze your data anywhere
- **PWA installable** — add it to your phone's home screen, no app store needed

Setup takes under 30 seconds. Enter your name, pick your currency, set an optional savings goal, and you're ready to go.

Whether you're paying off debt, building an emergency fund, or simply trying to spend less than you earn — this tracker gives you the clarity to make it happen.

**Download once. Use forever. Your finances, finally under control.**
